Our Story

Wat da Pho is a family-owned business proudly serving authentic Vietnamese cuisine, specializing in Pho noodle soup and offering a variety of dishes including stir-fried lo mein, rice dishes, and vegetarian options.

Wat da Pho proudly serves all who are looking to have a taste of our Vietnamese culture. Visit our Menu to learn more about our dishes.
